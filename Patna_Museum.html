City of historical and cultural importance!

Patna, the capital of Bihar, is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world. The city has a rich history, and has been a centre of learning and culture since the days of the Maurya and Gupta empires.

Today, Patna is a bustling metropolis, and is home to a number of educational institutions, as well as historical and cultural sites. The city is also a major economic hub, and is a great place to do business.

If you're looking for a city with a rich history and culture, and a bright future, then come to Patna!

Patna Museum

Patna museum is one of the oldest museums in India. It was established in 1917 by the then Governor of Bihar, Sir Edward Gait. The museum is located in the heart of the city of Patna, the capital of Bihar. The museum has a rich collection of artefacts ranging from the prehistoric to the modern times. The museum also houses a rare collection of coins, pottery, weapons, sculptures, paintings and textiles.

The museum has a rich collection of artefacts from the prehistoric period. These include tools and weapons used by the early humans, pottery, bones, and stone sculptures. The museum also has a collection of textiles from the Mughal period. The museum has a rich collection of coins from the medieval period.
