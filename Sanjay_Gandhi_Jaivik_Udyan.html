City of historical and cultural importance!

Patna, the capital of Bihar, is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world. The city has a rich history, and has been a centre of learning and culture since the days of the Maurya and Gupta empires.

Today, Patna is a bustling metropolis, and is home to a number of educational institutions, as well as historical and cultural sites. The city is also a major economic hub, and is a great place to do business.

If you're looking for a city with a rich history and culture, and a bright future, then come to Patna!

Sanjay Gandhi Jaivik Udyan

Sanjay Gandhi Jaivik Udyan, also known as Sanjay Gandhi Biological Park, is a 568-acre (2.29 km2) zoo and botanical garden located in Patna, Bihar, India. The park was inaugurated by then-Chief Minister of Bihar, Chandrashekhar Singh, on 16 August 1991. It is named after Sanjay Gandhi, the youngest son of Indira Gandhi.

The park is home to more than 1,500 animals, birds and reptiles, representing almost 130 species from around the world. The park also has a botanical garden with more than 2,000 species of plants and trees.

The park is open from 8:00 am to 5:00 pm, and entry is free for children under the age of 12. Admission for adults is Rs. 20 for Indians and Rs. 200 for foreigners.
